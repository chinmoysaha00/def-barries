WHAT HAPPENS NEXT WITH ACTIV8

call from project manager

Your dedicated Activ8 Project Manager will follow up on your solar enquiry with a phone call to confirm your information, grant eligibility, solar suitability and answer any questions you may have about the Activ8 process.

DESKTOP SURVEY

Following on from your enquiry, your Project Manager has conducted a desktop survey to understand the feasibility of your home and how many panels you may be able to fit on your roof and followed up with this quotation.

AGREEMENT & CONTRACT

If you decide to proceed with this quotation, your Project Manager will send you a full contract. Upon signing and securing your installation with a deposit, Johanna will start the process of your SEAI grant (if eligible) and pass your details to our Technical Survey team.

TECHNICAL CHECK

Your Project Manager will arrange for a solar survey engineer to call to your home and conduct a full technical survey. Full pick list and RAMS will be put together for our Installation Department so that your installation will be as seamless as possible.

INSTALLATION

Your dedicated Activ8 Project Manager will organise for our Installation Department to book in your installation. Your Activ8 crew will have two electricians and two roofers on-site to install your new Activ8 solar PV system in just one day.

SOLAR SAVINGS

Your dedicated Activ8 Project Manager will still be there for you after installation, help you with any referrals and any other enquiries you may have. Other than that, it's time for you to enjoy your solar savings, safe in the knowledge that we'll be there if you need us.
