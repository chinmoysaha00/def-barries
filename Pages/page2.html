Who we are?

Founded in 2007, we have over 16 years experience supplying solar panels across Ireland. As a partner of SSE Airtricity, you know your solar system is in safe hands. Now and into the future. As Ireland's solar experts, we will always be here for you.

What we do?

We install solar PV systems for homeowners all over Ireland. We also install solar for some of Ireland's leading companies and institutions including Microsoft, Intel, HSE, Johnson & Johnson, Musgraves along with utility scale solar projects up to 10,000 panels at a time.

03

How we do it?

Two of our six Core Values are Quality & Safety and they are at the heart of everything we do. This is reflected in our ISO 9001 (Quality), 140001 (Environment) and 45001 (Health & Safety) accreditations and our recent 100% Achilles audit.
